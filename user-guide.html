Logging in

  1. Open the BeFitMe website.
  2. Click Login in the top-right corner.
  3. Enter your username or email address and password.
  4. Click Log in.

Managing specialties

From the Admin page, open the Specialties section to view, add, or delete specialties.

Managing institutions

From the Admin page, open the Institutions section to view, add, or delete institutions.

Managing users

From the Admin page, use the Invite New User, Users, and related controls to manage dashboard access.

Exporting data

From the Data Export page, choose CSV or JSON and then download survey, frailty, or activity data.
