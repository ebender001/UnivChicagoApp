BeFitMe Watch App Guide

Install, set up, and use the BeFitMe Watch app with the same website look and feel.

Back to Dashboard Website User Guide

Requirements

  • Apple Watch running watchOS 10 or later
  • iPhone running iOS 17 or later
  • BeFitMe Watch installed on your iPhone
  • Bluetooth enabled and both devices signed in to the same Apple ID

Install the App

From iPhone

  1. Open the Watch app on your iPhone.
  2. Go to My Watch → Available Apps.
  3. Find BeFitMe Watch and tap Install.

From Apple Watch

  1. Press the Digital Crown to open the app grid or list.
  2. Open the App Store on Apple Watch.
  3. Search for BeFitMe Watch and tap Get.

Set Up on Apple Watch

  1. Open BeFitMe Watch on your iPhone and sign in or create an account.
  2. Open BeFitMe Watch on your Apple Watch.
  3. Follow on-screen prompts to grant permissions like Health, Motion, and Location.
  4. Choose your preferred units, themes, and notification settings.

Navigation & Basics

  • Open the app by pressing the Digital Crown and tapping the BeFitMe Watch icon.
  • Use on-screen buttons and the Digital Crown to scroll and select options.
  • Swipe from the left edge or tap the back button to return.
  • Touch and hold for context menus when available.

Exact controls may vary by feature and watchOS version.

Key Features

Quick Glance

See important data at a glance. The main screen shows live stats and recent updates.

Logging & Tracking

Start, pause, and stop sessions directly from your wrist. Progress syncs automatically.

Voice & Dictation

Tap the microphone to dictate notes and review entries before saving.

Customization

Use the Watch app on iPhone to adjust units, themes, and default views.

Complications

  1. Touch and hold your watch face, then tap Edit.
  2. Swipe to Complications.
  3. Select a slot and choose BeFitMe Watch.
  4. Press the Digital Crown to save your changes.

Available styles vary by watch face and device size.

Notifications

  • Open the Watch app on your iPhone and choose Notifications → BeFitMe Watch.
  • Allow notifications and select your alert style.
  • On Apple Watch, swipe down from the top to open Notification Center.

For important updates, enable Time Sensitive notifications if supported.

Troubleshooting

App not installing

  • Ensure the iPhone app is installed and you are signed in.
  • Install from Watch app → My Watch → Available Apps.
  • Restart both your iPhone and Apple Watch.
  • Verify both devices meet the requirements.

Data not syncing

  • Confirm Bluetooth and Wi-Fi are enabled.
  • Open BeFitMe Watch on iPhone to refresh sync.
  • Force-quit the watch app on Apple Watch and reopen it.

Missing permissions

  • On iPhone, go to Settings → Privacy & Security and re-enable the needed permissions.
  • If using Health data, check Health → Privacy → Apps → BeFitMe Watch.

Warning: If issues persist, unpair and re-pair your Apple Watch as a last resort. Back up your data first.

Privacy

  • Only necessary permissions are requested.
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest where applicable.
  • You can delete your data or account from the iPhone app.

See our Privacy Policy for full details.

FAQ

Does BeFitMe Watch work without my iPhone nearby?

Some features work independently on Apple Watch. Others require the iPhone for connectivity or processing.

How do I change units or theme?

Open the Watch app on iPhone → BeFitMe Watch → Settings, or adjust settings within the iPhone app.

How do I export or delete my data?

Open the iPhone app → Settings → Data Management to export or delete your data.
